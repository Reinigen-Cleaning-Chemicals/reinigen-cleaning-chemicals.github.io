Who We Are

Reinigen Cleaning Chemicals & Supplies (A Brand of Mrinesh Associates)

Reinigen Cleaning Chemicals & Supplies is a home-grown manufacturer of cleaning chemicals and supplies based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. Founded in 2010, we make practical, reliable products for homes, offices, hospitals and industrial spaces.

From a small beginning with just two employees and five products, we now operate two manufacturing units (Coimbatore and Erode), have around 15 employees and serve about 500 customers across retailers, wholesalers and B2B clients. Reinigen is ISO - certified and produces eco-friendly formulations designed for everyday use.

Our History

2010 - THE BEGINNING: Began as a small-scale business with a small manufacturing unit and five basic cleaning items.

EARLY YEARS WITH STEADY GROWTH: As customers appreciated our quality and value, we expanded our product range and production capacity.

POST - COVID AWARENESS: The pandemic increased public focus on hygiene, demand for dependable cleaning products rose and we responded by strengthening supply and quality control.

TODAY: Two manufacturing units, a broader product range, a committed team and a growing base of loyal customers.

Our Motto & Philosophy

"Clean living, healthy living."

Just as a home is incomplete without cooking, a home cannot be truly healthy without regular cleaning. Good hygiene prevents disease, brings peace of mind and improves quality of life.

Reinigen exists to make cleanliness simple and dependable for every household and workplace. Trust is fundamental to our business philosophy.

Our Vision & Commitment

We aim to make trusted hygiene products accessible and affordable across India. Quality and safety are central to everything we do - our ISO certification underlines this commitment.

We follow documented quality checks from raw material sourcing to final packing to deliver consistent, safe products. We are also committed to eco-friendly practices in our formulations and continually work to improve product performance while keeping people and the environment in mind. We are actively engaged in day-to-day operations, while our R&D team continuously works on innovation through close interaction with customers to understand their needs and pain points.
